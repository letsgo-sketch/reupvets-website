Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Introduction

ReUpVets ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using ReUpVets, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, password, first name, last name
  • Profile Information: Military branch, rank, profile photo, bio
  • Service History: Units served in, bases, years of service
  • Content: Posts, comments, messages, photos you share
  • Event Information: Events you create or RSVP to

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features used, interactions within the app
  • Push Notification Tokens: To deliver notifications to your device

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data (unless you choose to share it in posts)
  • Contacts or address book
  • Financial or payment information (supporter subscriptions are handled by Apple/Google)
  • Biometric data

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Connect you with other veterans
  • Enable messaging between users
  • Display posts and community content
  • Send push notifications for messages, connection requests, and events
  • Provide crisis resources when relevant content is detected locally on your device
  • Improve and optimize the app
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

Crisis Detection

ReUpVets includes a feature that detects language that may indicate a user is in crisis. This detection happens entirely on your device - no data is sent to our servers or any third party. When detected, we display crisis resources including the Veterans Crisis Line (988).

How We Share Your Information

With Other Users

  • Your profile information (name, branch, rank, photo, bio) is visible to other verified veterans
  • Posts you create are visible to the community (or anonymous if you choose)
  • Messages are only visible to the recipients

With Service Providers

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, file storage, push notifications
  • Expo: App development and updates
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: App distribution

We Do NOT

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for targeted advertising

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe such action is necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect the safety of users or the public, or protect against legal liability.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication through Firebase
  • Database security rules limiting access to authorized users

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Your profile information will be deleted
  • Your posts may remain but will be anonymized
  • Messages in conversations with other users may remain visible to those users
  • We may retain certain information as required by law

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Update

You can access and update your profile information at any time through the app's settings.

Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion through the app settings. This will remove your profile and personal data from our systems.

Push Notifications

You can disable push notifications through your device settings at any time.

Opt-Out

You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

ReUpVets is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we discover that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information from our servers.

Veterans-Specific Considerations

We understand the sensitive nature of military service information. We are committed to:

  • Protecting your service history and veteran status
  • Never sharing your information with unauthorized parties
  • Providing a safe space for veteran-to-veteran connection

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app, updating the "Last Updated" date, and sending a push notification for significant changes.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@reupvets.com

Website: https://reupvets.com

California Residents

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to equal service and price

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@reupvets.com.

International Users

ReUpVets is operated in the United States. If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States.
